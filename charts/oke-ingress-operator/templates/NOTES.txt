1) Operator status:
   kubectl -n kube-system get deploy oke-ingress-operator

2) Logs:
   kubectl -n kube-system logs deploy/oke-ingress-operator -f

3) Labeler check:
   kubectl get nodes -l {{ .Values.labeler.labelKey }}={{ .Values.labeler.labelValue }} -o wide

4) Backend-sync:
   Annotate your Service with:
   oci.oraclecloud.com/node-label-selector: "{{ .Values.labeler.labelKey }}={{ .Values.labeler.labelValue }}"
